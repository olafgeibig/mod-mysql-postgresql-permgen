TVSmiles Jobs {{version.pom_version}}

These are executable jobs to perform some administrative tasks. Do not execute jobs unless you exactly know what you're doing! Some of theses jobs perform massive updates to the database or send out notifications to a huge number of customers. Please be careful.

If you got here by accident, please forget about this page, erase your memory, burn your browser history and finally close this window. If you don't do so many kittens will die.

Important: Many jobs have got a 'dry run' option and won't really be executed unless the option is unchecked. During dry run everything is executed normally but without performing the real action, e.g. calling an external API. Always test your job in 'dry run' mode and check if the job finishes successfully with out any errors before unchecking 'dry run'.

Push

Contains several jobs to deal with push notifications to the user's mobile apps. This will make use of Urban Airship.

  • Tag Users. Tag users at Urban Airship so they can be part of segments.
  • Create Segment. Create a segment of devices at Urban Airship based on tags.
  • Push Notification. Push a notification to a whole segment.
